Apertus Audio Stage 2 — Ablation Report

Qualitative evaluation of speech understanding across 6 multilingual benchmarks

⚙

Experiment Setup

Both models are trained on the same 50/50 audio + text data mix (36.5 B audio tokens interleaved with text data) for continued pretraining from the Apertus-1.5 8B Stage 1 checkpoint. The only difference is whether the model learns from audio tokens or ignores them.

⚖

Checkpoints

weight-0 — Audio loss weight = 0. Audio data is present in the training mix, but the loss on audio tokens is zeroed out. The model sees audio context but is not trained to predict audio tokens.

weight-1 — Audio loss weight = 1. Standard training where the model learns from both audio and text tokens equally. This is the full multimodal training objective.

♬

Tasks

Transcribe — Given an audio clip, the model produces a text transcription. Prompt: [audio] <|stt_transcribe|>. Measures speech recognition quality.

Continue — Given an audio clip, the model generates a natural text continuation of what was said. Prompt: [audio] <|stt_continue|>. Measures speech understanding and coherence.
